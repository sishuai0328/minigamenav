Monster Survivors

Battle endless waves of monsters in this thrilling survival game with unique abilities and upgrades!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, unlock powerful abilities, and see how long you can survive against the increasing difficulty.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose new abilities and upgrades when you level up
  • Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from different characters with unique starting abilities and playstyles.

Ability System

Unlock and upgrade various abilities to create powerful combinations.

Enemy Variety

Face different types of monsters with unique behaviors and abilities.

Progressive Difficulty

The game gets increasingly challenging the longer you survive.
